We created a stop motion video to show the history and process of Nishiyama Silk, a textile company nestled at the foot of the Japanese Alps. All collage elements are made out of their beautiful fabrics which originate in the land of the rising sun and expand to the runways of Paris and Milan. Made in collaboration with  MyORB .
